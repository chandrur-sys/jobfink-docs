# Apply for Measure – Document Submission Process

### Overview
After completing the voucher and target location configuration, users can upload required documents and apply for a measure through the Documents module.

Once the process is completed and the email is sent successfully, the Maßnahme status is updated automatically.

---

### Prerequisites
- Voucher and Target Location configuration must be completed.
- Location-specific Maßnahme must be active.
- User must have document upload permission.
- Required checklists must be configured.

---

### Step-by-Step Process

#### Step 1: Open Documents Tab
1. Navigate to the required Maßnahme.
2. Open the **Location-Specific Maßnahme**.
3. Click on the **Documents** tab.

**Expected Result:**  
Documents module is displayed.

---

#### Step 2: Select Required Checklists
1. Select the default checklist.
2. Select the **Location-Specific Checklist**.

**Expected Result:**  
Selected checklists are highlighted and activated.

---

#### Step 3: Upload Documents
1. Click on **Upload Document**.
2. Select the required files.
3. Complete the upload process.

**Expected Result:**  
Uploaded documents are listed successfully.

---

#### Step 4: Save Document Details
1. Click the **Save** button.

**Expected Result:**  
Document details are saved without validation errors.

---

#### Step 5: Apply for Measure
1. Click on the **Apply for Measure** button.

**Expected Result:**  
Email popup window is displayed.

---

#### Step 6: Verify Email Popup
1. Review the email popup.
2. Verify that uploaded documents are displayed as attachments.

**Expected Result:**  
All uploaded documents are visible in the popup.

---

#### Step 7: Send Email
1. Enter recipient email address (if required).
2. Click **Send Mail**.

**Expected Result:**  
- Email is sent successfully to the receiver.
- Documents are attached correctly.

---

#### Step 8: Verify Maßnahme Status
1. Navigate back to the Maßnahme details page.
2. Check the Maßnahme status.

**Expected Result:**  
Maßnahme status is updated to **Applied for Measure Number**.

---

#### Validation Points
- Both default and location-specific checklists are selectable.
- Documents upload without errors.
- Email popup shows correct attachments.
- Mail is delivered to the recipient.
- Status updates automatically after sending mail.

---

#### Exception Scenarios
- If documents are missing, system displays validation message.
- If email fails, status is not updated.
- If mandatory checklist is not selected, apply action is blocked.

---

#### Notes
- Ensure stable internet connection during document upload.
- Verify recipient email before sending.
- Contact system administrator if mail delivery fails.
