# Revision Process – Resubmit for Approval

### Step 1: Open Maßnahme with Applied for Approval Status
1. Navigate to the **Maßnahme** list.
2. Select the Maßnahme with status **Applied for Approval**.
3. Click on the Maßnahme to open it.

### Step 2: Revise Session Information
1. Go to the **Session Information** tab.
2. Click on **Revise as Directed**.
3. Click **Save and Continue**.
4. The system will navigate to the **Curriculum Details** screen.

### Step 3: Update Curriculum Details
1. Review and update the required curriculum details.
2. Click **Save and Continue**.
3. The system will redirect you to the **Documents** tab.

### Step 4: Upload Revision Documents
1. In the **Documents** tab, click on the required **Checklist**.
2. Select the **Revision Checklist**.
3. Upload all mandatory documents.
4. Click **Save**.
5. The uploaded documents will be displayed in the **Documents Table**.

### Step 5: Resubmit for Approval
1. Click on **Revision**.
2. The **Email Popup** will be displayed.

### Step 6: Email Details
1. The uploaded documents will be automatically fetched in the email description.
2. In the **To** field, the receiver’s email ID will be auto-populated.
3. The **From** field will display the logged-in user’s email ID.

### Step 7: Send Revision for Approval
1. Review the email content.
2. Click **Send Message**.
3. The email will be sent successfully to the receiver.
4. The Maßnahme status will be updated to **Revision**.

---

**Expected Result**
- Revision documents are uploaded successfully.
- Approval email is sent to the receiver.
- Maßnahme status changes to **Revision**.
