# Apply for Approval Process

After creating the Maßnahme, you must complete all the sub-modules and submit the Maßnahme for the approval process.

---
### Maßnahme Information

**Maßnahme Type**

Select the required Maßnahme type:
Group  
One-on-One Coaching  
Internship  

**Maßnahme Objective**

Select the appropriate Maßnahme objective from the available list.

**Other Fields**

Fill in all remaining mandatory fields in the form.

---

**Action Buttons**

After filling in the details, use the following buttons:

**Save**

Click **Save** to save the entered details.

**Save & Continue**

Click **Save & Continue** to save the details and go to the next section.

**Cancel**

Click **Cancel** to exit without saving.

**Skip**

Click **Skip** to move to the next section without filling the details.

### Session Information

The selected Maßnahme Type values are reflected in the Session Information sub-tab.

---

**Coaching Units and Pricing**

Enter the respective coaching units.
Price/Unit and Max. Revenue are auto-populated based on the entered units.
The same pricing logic is applied for One-on-One Coaching and Internship.

---

**Internship Supervision**

Internship Supervision follows the same pricing process as Internship.

**Validation Rules**

Toggle ON: Value is calculated in UEs (45 minutes).
Toggle OFF: Value is calculated in hours.

---

**Min and Max Constraints**

Enter the minimum and maximum values for:

Group sessions  
One-on-One Coaching sessions  

These values define the allowed session limits.

---

**Company Plan**

Fill in the following details carefully:

**Number of UEs (Group Sessions)**
Enter how many UEs are contacted per day.

**Number of Group Sessions**

This field is auto-populated based on:
Total Group UEs
Number of UEs per Group Session

**One-on-One Coaching and Internship
The same calculation flow is applied for:
One-on-One Coaching
Internship

---

**Submission**

Before proceeding, make sure:

All required fields are filled.
Pricing is auto-populated correctly.
Min and Max values are entered.
Company Plan details are verified.

Click **Save & Continue** to move to the next section.

### Curriculum Details

**Overview**

After completing the **Session Information** section, click **Save & Continue**.  
The system will navigate you to the **Curriculum Details** page.

On This page, users can configure session details for:

Group Sessions  
One-on-One Coaching  
Internship Sessions  

Users can enter session names, add descriptions, and review the auto-populated duration.

---

**Page Sections**

The Curriculum Details page consists of the following sections:

1. Group Session

This section is used to configure group-based training sessions.

**Fields:**

**Session Name**: Enter the name of the group session.
**Duration per Session**: Auto-populated Session Information >Company Plan > Number of UEs Group sessions
**Description**: Optional field to add session details.

---

2. One-on-One Coaching

This section is used for individual coaching sessions.

**Fields:**

**Session Name**: Enter the coaching session name.
**Duration per Session**: Auto-populated Session Information >Company Plan >Number of UEs One-on-One sessions
**Description**: Optional field for additional information.

---

3. Internship Session

This section is used for internship-related training sessions.

**Fields:**

**Session Name**: Enter the internship session name.
**Duration per Session**: Auto-populated Session Information >Company Plan >Number of UEs Internship sessions

**Description**: Optional field to describe the internship session.

---

**Duration per Session**

The **Duration per Session** field is automatically populated.
The value is based on:
 - Session Information >Company Plan >Coaching type Configuraation

---

**Uploading Curriculum Documents**

If any supporting documents are required:

1. Navigate to the **Documents** tab.
2. Open **Documents > Other Documents**.
3. Upload the relevant curriculum files.

These documents will be linked to the Maßnahme.

---

**Saving Curriculum Details**

After entering all required information:

1. Review the session names and descriptions.
2. Verify the auto-populated duration values.
3. Click the **Save** button.

**Result**

All entered values are saved successfully.
The curriculum details are updated in the system.
The Maßnahme configuration process continues.

---

**Validation Rules**

Duration per Session must be auto-generated.
Uploaded documents must follow supported file formats.

---

### Apply for Approval – Document Submission Process

#### Step 1: Save Curriculum Details
1. Enter the required curriculum details.
2. Click **Save and Continue**.
3. The system will redirect you to the **Documents** tab.

#### Step 2: Add Checklist and Upload Documents
1. In the **Documents** tab, click on the required **Checklist**.
2. Select the appropriate **Checklist Title** for the Maßnahme.
3. Upload the required documents.
4. The uploaded documents will be displayed in the **Documents Table**.

#### Step 3: Apply for Approval
1. Click on **Apply for Approval** Button in Documents Tab.
2. An **Email Popup** will be displayed.

#### Step 4: Email Details
1. In the **To** field, enter the receiver’s email ID.
   - The receiver’s email ID will be auto-populated based on the selected person in Fachkundige Stelle.
2. The **From** field will display the logged-in user’s email ID.
3. The uploaded documents will be automatically added to the **Mail Description**.

#### Step 5: Send for Approval
1. Review the email details.
2. Click **Send Message**.
3. The email will be sent to the receiver successfully.
4. The Maßnahme status will be updated to **Applied for Approval**.

---

**Expected Result**
- Documents are uploaded successfully.
- Approval email is sent to the receiver.
- Maßnahme status changes to **Applied for Approval**.
